Canyon Ride

The Orange River Canyon is a 30 mile long canyon that takes you on an incredible journey through the beautiful Orange River Mountains. These lush, green mountains offer a peaceful and serene ride through nature at its best.

Forest Adventure

If the peaceful ride isn't for you and you're looking for a little more adventure, the Forest Adventure route is perfect for you. Traveling through the majestic Hilton Forest offers both the beauty of this ancient forest but also the exhilirating rush of the river's many rapids, twists and turns.

Wildlife Safari

If you're looking for the ultimate river rafting package, the Wildlide Safari is the place to be. This grand tour of everything the Orange River will take you through amazing little forests, hair-raising rapids and, of course, the incredible variety of wildlife that the Orange River has to offer.
