Site Plan

Site Name

Lucky Whiskers

Purpose

During the pandemic when many people were isolated at home, they started looking for companions in their pets. As a result, in 2020 and 2021, pet stores and retailers saw a massive boost in sales. The demand for pets is still high but many potential buyers do not want to make a trip to their local pet store to buy one. What do they do? With your endorsement, they would use our unique pet adoption website that, in collaboration with local pet stores and pet adoption agencies, will allow clients to view the stores’ collection of pets, select one for adoption and have their new animal companion delivered to their doors all without leaving the comfort of their couches.

Target Audience

Our research shows that the majority of our potential clients are single. This is because, as the pandemic continues to isolate us, they are searching for an animal companion to keep them company. Our research also indicates that our potential clients are either young (18-25 years old) or elderly (60+ years old). Therefore, the young and the elderly who are single will be our target audience. Here are two scenarios showing why potential customers will visit our site:

  • They will be searching to adopt a pet to keep them company. Our site will give them a detailed catalogue showing their options.
  • They will be looking for a way to adopt a pet without having to leave their homes. Our site will give them the option of choosing to have their chosen pet delivered to their doors.
