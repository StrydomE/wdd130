Lacy

The cutest little Beagle you will ever find, Lacy was rescued from her previous home in Montana. She is extremely friendly and loves playing with small children. She would make a wonderful new addition to your home.

Oscar

This ginger tomcat loves to play. String, lasers, toy mice, you name it, Oscar loves to play with it. Rescued from the streets of New York, he would make an excellent companion to anyone who likes to play with animals.

George

This little parakeet is just bursting with energy. He sings and hops around if you pay him the slightest attention. He might even nibble your finger affectionately if you put it close to his cage.

Floppy

As her name suggests, Floppy loves to flop around. She loves hugs and will curl up with anyone who is willig to spend some time with her. Rescued from Atlanta, she is the perfect companion for anyone who likes warm hugs.
