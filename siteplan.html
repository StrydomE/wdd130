Site Name

The Rapids Rafting Company

Purpose

The purpose of the website will be to attract customers for The Rapids Rafting Company. They provide high quality whitewater rafting adventures for people of all ages and levels of experience/ability. Safety is their number one priority, and they pride themselves on the fact that they have only had two accidents in their fifty year existence. Their rafting adventures/trails are affordable, they employ experienced guides and their equipment is of the highest quality. Our website will need to include all of these details, honing in on their safety standards, affordability and their quality services.

Target Audience

Our demographic research shows that customers of whitewater rafting companies are generally below the age of 40, have a moderate income, Class II experience and travel in groups. Families with adolescent children are also frequent customers. This is our target audience and we need to cater for both adventerous youth and families. The demographic also shows that most people will access our website from a computer or laptop. Here are a few scenarios describing why these two groups of people would visit our site:

  • They will want to book reservations, usually for the weekend or vacation trips. Our website will need to offer them a way to make this reservation.
  • After booking their reservations, they will most likely wish to pay online. Our website needs to give them the option of securely paying online.
  • They will visit our website with questions concerning the quality and safety standards of the company's service. Our website needs to provide this information, including all of the company's safety and quality certifications. A few testimonials will also be useful.
  • They will also be curious as to the variety of services that the company offers. Our website will need to give a detailed description of the various trails/routes that the company offers. We should also include the levels of experience for each route/trail.
